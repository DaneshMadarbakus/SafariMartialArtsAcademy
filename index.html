About

I am a qualified Judo instructor. I have been training for over ten years in Judo, Muay Thai and Boxing. With the high increasing rate of crime in London specially amongst the youth, my aim is to spread discipline alongside healthy living through Martial Arts. I have a program designed for the young and the youth to learn about self discipline, self control, self defence, healthy living, healthy eating, and respect for one another. The way this will be taught and spread is though Martial Arts specifically Art of Muay Thai and Judo.

This program is also designed for all adults who don't have the time and commitment to spare to go to the gym, who don't have the knowledge or resources to not only get fit but also learn self defence through the beautiful art of Muay Thai/Boxing and Judo.

My program will make all that possible. It will give the youth guidance and not only skills to look after themselves but most importantly life skills they will adapt to their own as they're developing into becoming adults. And for the adults, the membership for the gym, sacrifice of time, lack of knowledge and resourses are no longer a problem as I will simply come to you and share the wealth of knowledge I have gained through many years of studying, training and competing.
